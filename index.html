Primates

Primates are a special group of animals that includes monkeys, apes, and humans, and they are known for being smart and good at using their hands.
Gorillas are the biggest apes and live in families with a strong leader called a silverback, who helps protect the group.
Monkeys are different because they have tails and live in many places around the world, swinging in trees and working together in groups.
Both gorillas and monkeys are important to nature, but some are in danger because people cut down forests and hunt them.
